THE YOYO'S STORY YoYo’s started in 2018 as a frozen yogurt shop, evolving into a coffee spot during the 2020 pandemic. By 2023, it added a bar, becoming a versatile space that transitions from a cozy café by day to a lively bar at night, embodying community and innovation.
WELCOME TO YOYO'S At the heart of Mahomet, YoYo's is your go-to spot to unwind and enjoy great vibes, whether it's day or night. As evening rolls in, the atmosphere shifts from laid-back to lively, with expertly crafted cocktails and mouthwatering flatbreads that will leave you craving more. We’re all about supporting local talent, so live music is coming soon—stay tuned! Whether you're after a cozy cappuccino or a fun night out, YoYo's Coffee and Bar has it all. Coffee, cocktails, and nothing but good vibes.
